A note to the reader:

Since you made it to my site - welcome, I’m glad you’re here. This site, meechio, serves as my creative half. A place that allows me to collect pictures, stories, personal memories, thoughts, and whatever else I want.

I made the decision to publish this online, because the internet has the ability to cultivate communities of people and provide a space to share thoughts, ideas, and stories. While this platform is mainly about sharing my voice, I would love to hear yours as well.

But most importantly, I created meechio to function as a time-capsule. I want a piece of myself (my authentic self) to be established so that in the future, myself and those I love, can look through my past and relive my memories via the creations on this site.

Now of course this site requires a degree of diligence on my end. For that reason, I’m putting this promise in public domain to hold myself accountable: I will consistently add to the meechio capsule and keep it running for as long as I’m alive. A bit of a bold statement, but I like to hold true on promises.

If you have read this far, I truly appreciate it.

I hope you are able to pull something of use from meechio, and please feel free to reach out and share any thoughts.

Cheers, Sebastian
