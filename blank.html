Since you made it to my site - welcome, I’m glad you’re here. This site, meechio, serves as my creative half. A place that allows me to collect pictures, stories, personal memories, thoughts, and whatever else I want.

-

I made the decision to publish this online, because the internet has the ability to cultivate communities of people and provide a space to share thoughts, ideas, and stories. While this platform is mainly about sharing my voice, I would love to hear yours as well.

-

But most importantly, I created meechio to function as a time-capsule. I want a piece of myself (my authentic self) to be established so that in the future, myself and those I love, can look through my past and experience my memories through the creations on this site.
